A Brief Bio

By Jakob Stickles

Before moving to Seattle for college, I grew up in Arlington, Washington. I discovered my passion for software development after joining my high school's FIRST robotics team (Go NeoBots!). While there, I learned countless things about team management, system prototyping, and robotics hardware. After getting accepted into the UW and joining the STARS program, I began pursuing computer engineering.

Why Computer Engineering?

I love using software to bring ideas into the real world. As computer hardware advances, technologies like LED displays, autonomous robots, and IoT devices are pushed to achieve increasingly impressive capabilities. Since I find the interface between hardware and software fascinating, computer engineering has been a great field to pursue. I often daydream about building an unconventional computer of my own design; a fully mechanical, internet-connected digital computer would be an entertaining project!
